About Us

Miniland Academy English School, located in Kerabari 7, Dangi, Morang, Nepal, is dedicated to providing high-quality education in a nurturing and supportive environment. We are committed to fostering academic excellence, personal character development, and the holistic growth of our students, equipping them with the skills and confidence to face the challenges of the future.
